About Me

My name is Jonathan, and I'm a web developer. I've always been an early adopter, and a constant tinkerer; running custom ROM's on my phones, modifying programs to my liking, and overclocking hardware. I found my passion for programming through my love of problem-solving and logic, combined with a desire to innovate.

If I'm not writing code or building websites, I am with my wife, daughter, and new-born son. Everything I do, I do for them. We love Settlers of Catan, strategy games and logic puzzles, Game of Thrones, Rick and Morty, Harry Potter, and everything else 'geeky'.

I write a lot of JavaScript (jQuery, ReactJS, NodeJS), HTML, and CSS/SASS/SCSS. When it comes to development I often find myself pulled towards the front-end stack. Responsiveness, compatibility, and the psychology of design are my first thoughts when creating an application or website.

Having an understanding of the functionality and implementation best practices of web servers, API's, and databases helps me create seamless experiences for the user.
